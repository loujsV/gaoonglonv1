Our Story

Gao Ong Lon —
A Name You Can Trust

Gao Ong Lon was founded with one simple mission: bringing the finest Vietnamese ST25 rice directly to families in Southern California. Named after generations of dedication, every grain we sell carries the authentic taste of Vietnam's Mekong Delta.


ST25 rice earned the title of "World's Best Rice" at the World's Best Rice Contest — and we're proud to bring it right to your doorstep here in Westminster, California.
